why choose us?

Automatically log and tally the number of vehicles in your footage...

Vehicle counting is the process of detecting and logging the number of vehicles that enter a defined zone or cross a defined line. This system is highly accurate and efficient and can be used in various applications such as toll booths, parking lots, and traffic management.

Solutions

Where is it useful?

toll booths

The system can be installed at toll booths to automatically collect tolls from vehicles passing through, reducing traffic congestion and improving the efficiency of toll collection.

read more

Highways & Roads

The system can be installed on Highways & Roads to monitor traffic flow, speed and vehicle types, allowing authorities to make informed decisions about traffic management and road maintenance.

read more

Parking Lots

The system can be installed in parking lots to monitor the number of vehicles entering and existing the parking lot and to assist with parking space management.

read more

Event Venues

The system can be installed at event venues such as sports stadiums and concert halls to monitor the number of vehicles entering and exiting the venue and to assist with traffic management.

read more

Border Checkpoints

The system can be installed at border checkpoints to monitor the number of vehicles entering and exiting the country, helping to manage border security and immigration.

Industrial Areas

The system can be installed in industrial areas to monitor the number and types of vehicles which are entering and exiting the area, helping to improve safety and security.
